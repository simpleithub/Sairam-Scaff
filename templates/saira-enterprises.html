Scaffolding Spans

Scaffolding Spans are robust horizontal members used to create temporary working platforms and walkways on construction sites. They provide a safe, stable base for workers and equipment at elevated heights.

Floor Form

Floor Forms are temporary formwork systems designed to support concrete slabs and beams during pouring and curing. They ensure accurate slab dimensions and smooth finishes for high-quality concrete surfaces.

View More

Angle, Adjuster Floor Forms

Angle Floor Forms are specialized steel formwork systems used in beam and slab edge formations during concrete construction. These forms ensure precise alignment, durability, and structural strength, making them essential for high-quality construction projects. Adjuster Floor Forms are used alongside to accommodate varying lengths and provide seamless edge transitions.

Cup Lock Systems - Vertical and Ledger

Cup Lock Systems are modular scaffolding systems featuring vertical standards and horizontal ledgers. Known for their high strength, easy assembly, and versatility, they’re widely used in construction, maintenance, and infrastructure projects.

Column Box

Column Boxes are formwork systems specifically designed for casting concrete columns of varying sizes and shapes. They ensure accurate dimensioning, smooth finishes, and structural integrity for vertical elements.

H-Frame

H-Frames are sturdy scaffolding systems used for creating stable working platforms in vertical construction projects. Their rigid structure ensures safety and ease of access at elevated work areas.
